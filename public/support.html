Quick Start Guide

Installing the Add-in (IT Administrators)

  1. Sign in to Microsoft 365 Admin Center
  2. Navigate to Settings → Integrated apps → Add-ins
  3. Click Deploy Add-in and select Upload custom apps
  4. Choose I have a manifest file (XML) and upload the manifest file
  5. Select who gets access (specific users/groups or everyone)
  6. Click Deploy and wait up to 24 hours for deployment to complete

Using the Add-in (End Users)

  1. Open Microsoft Word or PowerPoint
  2. Go to Insert → Add-ins → My Add-ins
  3. Click on Rhythm Icons to launch the taskpane
  4. Sign in with your Microsoft 365 account when prompted
  5. Search for icons, customize size/color, and click to insert

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: The add-in isn't appearing in my Office apps. What should I do?

A: After your IT administrator deploys the add-in, it can take up to 24 hours to appear. Try these steps:

  • Close and reopen Word/PowerPoint
  • Check if you're signed in with your organizational account (not a personal Microsoft account)
  • Verify with your IT admin that the add-in was deployed to your user or group
  • Clear your Office cache: %LOCALAPPDATA%\Microsoft\Office\16.0\Wef\ (Windows) or ~/Library/Containers/com.microsoft.Word/Data/Library/Caches/ (Mac)
Q: I'm getting a sign-in error. How do I fix this?

A: Sign-in issues are usually related to authentication. Try these steps:

  • Ensure you're using your organizational Microsoft 365 account (personal accounts are not supported)
  • Clear your browser cookies and cache
  • Try signing out of Office and signing back in
  • Check if your organization has conditional access policies that might be blocking the add-in
  • Contact your IT administrator to verify Azure AD app permissions are properly configured
Q: Can I upload my own custom icons?

A: Yes! The add-in supports bulk icon upload:

  • Click the Upload Icons button in the taskpane
  • Select one or more SVG files from your computer
  • Choose a category for the icons
  • Preview the icons and click Add to Library
  • Custom icons are stored per tenant and only visible to users in your organization

Note: Icons must be in SVG format with a standard viewBox (0 0 24 24) and 2px stroke width for best results.

Q: Icons aren't inserting into my document. What's wrong?

A: If icons aren't inserting, check:

  • You have edit permissions for the document
  • The document isn't in protected or restricted editing mode
  • You're not in Read Mode (Word) or Slide Show mode (PowerPoint)
  • Try clicking on a different part of the document before inserting
  • Check the browser console (F12) for error messages and send them to support
Q: How do I change icon colors in PowerPoint?

A: PowerPoint icons support theme colors:

  • Click the color selector at the bottom of the taskpane before inserting
  • Choose from PowerPoint theme colors (Accent1-6, Text1-2, Background1-2)
  • After inserting, you can further customize colors by right-clicking the icon and selecting Format Picture
  • Icons will automatically adapt to your presentation's theme colors
Q: Can I use these icons in commercial projects?

  • Client deliverables and presentations

  • Internal business documents

Q: How do I remove the add-in?

A: To remove the add-in:

For end users:

  • In Word/PowerPoint, go to Insert → Add-ins → My Add-ins
  • Right-click on Rhythm Icons and select Remove

For IT administrators:

  • Go to Microsoft 365 Admin Center
  • Navigate to Settings → Integrated apps
  • Find Rhythm Icons and click Remove

System Requirements

  • Office Applications: Word 2016 or later, PowerPoint 2016 or later (Desktop, Online, or Mobile)
  • Operating Systems: Windows 10+, macOS 10.14+, iOS 12+, Android 6.0+
  • Browser: Microsoft Edge, Google Chrome, Safari, or Firefox (for Office Online)
  • Account: Microsoft 365 organizational account with Word/PowerPoint license
  • Internet: Active internet connection required for authentication and icon library access

Troubleshooting

Icon appears blurry or pixelated

Try selecting a larger icon size before inserting. For PowerPoint, use 64px or 96px for better quality in presentations.

Add-in crashes or freezes

This is usually caused by browser cache issues. Try:

  1. Close all Office applications
  2. Clear your browser cache (the add-in uses your browser's rendering engine)
  3. Reopen Office and try again

Can't find a specific icon

Use the search bar at the top of the taskpane. You can search by:

  • Icon name (e.g., "heart", "diabetes")
  • Category (e.g., "Custom")
  • Related terms
