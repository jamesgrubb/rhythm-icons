Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 16, 2026

Introduction

Spectrum Science Communications, LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Rhythm Icons Library Office Add-in (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Authentication Data

When you sign in to the Service using Microsoft Azure Active Directory, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • Your name (if provided by your organization)
  • Your Azure AD tenant ID (organization identifier)
  • User object ID (unique identifier within your organization)

Usage Data

We collect information about how you use the Service, including:

  • Icons you search for and insert into documents
  • Custom icons you upload (stored per tenant)
  • Icon customization preferences (size, color)
  • Application logs and error reports

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate and authorize access to the Service
  • Provide tenant-specific icon libraries
  • Maintain and improve the Service
  • Troubleshoot technical issues
  • Comply with legal obligations

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in secure PostgreSQL databases hosted on Railway.app infrastructure. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Azure AD
  • Tenant data isolation
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your organization maintains an active subscription to the Service. When your organization ends its subscription:

  • Tenant-specific icon data is deleted within 30 days
  • Usage logs are retained for up to 90 days for troubleshooting
  • Authentication records are purged immediately

Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Railway.app (hosting), Microsoft Azure (authentication)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

Tenant Isolation

If you are part of an organization using the Service:

  • Your data is isolated from other tenants
  • Only users within your organization can access your tenant's custom icons
  • IT administrators in your organization may have access to usage information

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Objection: Object to processing of your data

To exercise these rights, please contact your organization's IT administrator or email us at privacy@spectrum-science.com.

GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Contract Performance: To provide the Service to your organization
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve and maintain the Service
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with applicable laws

Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third-Party Services

The Service integrates with Microsoft Office and Azure Active Directory. Please refer to Microsoft's privacy policy for information about how they handle your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
