## What is Rhythm Icons?

- **30+ curated icons** for medical and scientific content
- **Customizable** - change size, color, and style
- **Upload custom icons** for your organization
- **Works in Word & PowerPoint** (Desktop, Online, and Mobile)

## Getting Started

**For IT Administrators:**

1. Visit: **https://rhythm-icons-production.up.railway.app**
2. Download the manifest file
3. Deploy via Microsoft 365 Admin Center (takes 5 minutes)
4. Detailed instructions: https://rhythm-icons-production.up.railway.app/support

**For End Users:**

Once your IT admin deploys the add-in, it will automatically appear in Word and PowerPoint. Users simply:
1. Open Word or PowerPoint
2. Go to Insert → Add-ins → Rhythm Icons
3. Sign in with Microsoft (first time only)
4. Start inserting icons!

## Key Features

✅ **Secure Azure AD authentication** - users sign in with their Microsoft 365 account
✅ **Tenant isolation** - your custom icons stay private to your organization
✅ **Theme colors** - icons adapt to your PowerPoint themes
✅ **Search & categories** - find icons quickly
